Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). For technical support, we typically respond within 24 hours.

Are your resources really free?

Yes! All our resources, including the PhD Planner, templates, and guides are 100% free. We believe in accessible education and supporting PhD students worldwide.

Can I request a specific resource?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new resources. Please use the "Feedback/Suggestions" option in the contact form or email us directly with your ideas.

Do you offer one-on-one PhD consulting?

While we primarily offer free resources, we do offer limited consulting services. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and availability.
